You're Not Alone

Engineering and CS students at Technion face intense academic pressure. Between demanding coursework, exams, and projects, it's easy to neglect physical health and lose touch with the people around you.

The challenge: Finding time to exercise, making friends outside your program, and managing stress. All while keeping up with your studies.

What if you could reduce stress, stay active, AND build a community, without adding another commitment to your schedule?
